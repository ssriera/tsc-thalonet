VPN Haste
VPN technology was designed to improve security, not performance. VPNs generally add significant overhead by adding a cumbersome security layer. We designed our own internet protocols, from the ground up, purely for the purpose of improving the network experience of gamers. Every millisecond matters to you, so we invented new methods of internet connectivity designed for a live, real-time internet.
With VPNs, you are generally connecting via the public internet that still has its own congestion issues. While it is possible to experience an improvement for players with particularly poor network routes, most players actually see a decrease in overall performance while using a VPN. We put your connection onto our own custom engineered network that chooses the best available paths for performance. Instead of you being stuck with whatever your ISP gives you, we route your connection on our own network that mixes the fastest public internet routes with our own private, dedicated fiber wavelengths carefully selected for low latency.
VPN technology can significantly increase the points of failure that occur in your network path. VPNs are unable to switch to new paths mid-game, so when something happens to the server you are connected to, it can cause additional problems and even disconnect you from the game. Our solution constantly tests different routes and in real time re-routes your game network data onto optimal paths. Due to this resilient feature, our solution protects you from all the common issues that cause connectivity issues today including network routers that get congested with overloaded customer data at peak times and internet backbone equipment failure.
